Alpha channel estimation

Alpha channel can be estimated when a movie is exported. You can render effects on other screen without addition. It is useful to play effects on 2D game.

You can save effects as AVI Format with an alpha channel. It is useful for creating a movie on a video editing software such an AfterEffects.

Play on Unity WebGL.
