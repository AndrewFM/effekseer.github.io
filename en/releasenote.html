Release note

1.20

Alpha channel estimation

Alpha channel can be estimated when a movie is exported. You can render effects on other screen without addition. It is useful to show effects on 2D game.

Off On

The function to save effects as AVI Format with alpha

You can save effects as AVI Format with an alpha channel. It is useful for creating a movie on a video editing software such AfterEffects.

Many sample effects

More than 20 sample effects are added. You can download these from the official website, too.

Improvement of Plugin for Unity

Distortion and Asset bundle now work. improved correspondence with multi platform(iOS, Android, WebGL) .

Play on Unity WebGL.

Changed hot keys

Hot keys take after AfterEffects.

Bug fixes

Fixed many bugs.
