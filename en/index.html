Effekseer is a tool for making a beautiful visual effects for games easily.

Effekseer is a free, open-source program for making particle effects for games. The effects made with the program can be exported as 2D sprite sheets, or played as a 3D effects on games.

Effects can be played on games with DirectX or OpenGL by embeding a runtime, and also on unity by UnityPackage.
