Visual Effect Creating tool
Effekseer

Download

Effekseer is an easy creating tool of beautiful visual effects for games.

Effekseer is a free, open-source program. You can use effects as 2D Animations or 3D effects incorporated into your games.

Effects are shown on games with DirectX or OpenGL by embeding a runtime, and also on games with unity by UnityPackage. And furthermore, effects are available on Windows, Mac OS X, Linux, iOS, Android, Emscripten, and so on.
